Logistics

Please come on time at 10am tomorrow! Come to 370 Jay St—it’s above the Jay St-MetroTech subway station. There will be a sign-in table in the entryway that will point you to the 4th floor.

  • If you are an NYU student, you can enter and exit the building anytime. You will be given a name tag at the check-in desk—wear it for the entire hackathon so we can identify you.
  • If you are not an NYU student, you must bring a government ID and must have already RSVP’d on Eventbrite (or emailed us already).
  • Anyone who didn’t already RSVP and is not an NYU student will not be allowed to enter. We’re unfortunately at capacity.

If you arrive after 1:00 PM and are not a current NYU student, you’ll need to contact us (Anshula and Lachlan), and we might be able to check you in. But after 1:00 PM we cannot guarantee entry.

Groups, Workshops, & Food

You can work alone or in a group. We’ll have a grouping game at the beginning to meet collaborators; only  current ITP/IMA students can access shop tools  (laser cutters, 3D printers, etc). You’re welcome to bring your own tools.

We have a bunch of workshops lined up, covering games, 3D modeling/printing, AI, and physical computing—here’s the schedule.

Food: We are providing lunch and snacks throughout the day, but NOT dinner.

  • Bring your own dinner or plan to grab something during the dinner break, there’s tons of fast casual/restaurants nearby
  • If you leave the building for dinner, check-in will be open again from 6:00 PM–7:30 PM, so keep your name tag for re-entry.

What to bring

  • Laptop
  • Anything you need to build your stupid project: tools, materials
  • Your stupid ideas!!!
  • Your ID card—NYU ID for current students, or government ID for everyone else
