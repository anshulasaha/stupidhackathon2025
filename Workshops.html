Workshops

Our lovely workshop leaders were bribed into conducting these workshops in exchange for my agreement to clean their red bins at the end of the semester.

Can I get a Box? - Josh Zhong Did you know there are free online 3D modelling tools you can use to create simple enclosures for your stupid projects?
Bring your laptop and a friend with 3D printing access to learn more!

DIY Cybertruck Sonny Angel - Ryan Rotella and Fabrizio Guccione Ever been lonely at your laptop? Well, no more! Come make your own light-up 3D printed Cybertruck Sonny Angel.
It's just a little guy with an LED and a dream.

Make a Little Game - Alanna Okun We'll be talking about a couple of tools for making lightweight, flexible digital games / gamelike experiences quickly, like Twine, Bitsy, and Google Sheets (for real!).
No coding experience necessary.
